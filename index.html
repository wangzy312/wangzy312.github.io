About Me

Ziyue Wang received the Ph.D. degree in Computational Mathematics from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2025. He was supervised by Prof. Ya-Feng Liu and was a member of Optimization Group. His research interests include optimization algorithm and its applications to signal processing and wireless communications, massive random access in massive machine-type communications.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations or any topics of mutual interest!
